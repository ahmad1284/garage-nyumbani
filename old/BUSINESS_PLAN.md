# Business Plan: Garage Nyumbani (Mobile Auto Care)

## 1. Executive Summary
**Garage Nyumbani** is a premium mobile automotive service provider based in Zanzibar. We bring professional car maintenance and emergency repairs directly to the customer's doorstep—whether at home, the office, or roadside. By leveraging technology (AI diagnostics and automated WhatsApp reminders), we provide a seamless, transparent, and high-end service experience that traditional garages lack.

## 2. Problem Statement
*   **Inconvenience:** Car owners in Zanzibar often lose hours or entire days taking their vehicles to traditional garages.
*   **Lack of Transparency:** Customers often face hidden costs and unclear service reports.
*   **Emergency Gaps:** Limited reliable 24/7 roadside assistance for breakdowns.
*   **Poor Maintenance Tracking:** Most drivers forget their next service date, leading to preventable mechanical failures.

## 3. The Solution: Garage Nyumbani
*   **Doorstep Service:** Fully equipped mobile units that perform 80% of common maintenance tasks on-site.
*   **AI-Powered Diagnostics:** Using GenAI to analyze complex issues from user descriptions, providing immediate expert advice.
*   **Automated CRM:** A custom admin portal that tracks service history and sends automated WhatsApp reminders for upcoming maintenance.
*   **24/7 Emergency Dispatch:** A dedicated priority system for roadside assistance.

## 4. Target Market
*   **Busy Professionals:** Individuals who value time and prefer home/office service.
*   **Corporate Fleets:** Businesses requiring regular maintenance for multiple vehicles with minimal downtime.
*   **Tour Operators:** Ensuring tourist transport vehicles are always in peak condition.
*   **Expatriates & Premium Car Owners:** Customers seeking high-quality, reliable, and tech-forward service.

## 5. Revenue Model
*   **Service Fees:** Fixed pricing for standard maintenance (Oil changes, brake checks, tire recovery).
*   **Emergency Premiums:** Higher margins for 24/7 roadside assistance and priority dispatch.
*   **Parts Markup:** Revenue from high-quality replacement parts sourced through our network.
*   **Subscription Plans (Future):** Monthly "Peace of Mind" packages for regular fleet inspections.

## 6. Marketing Strategy
*   **Digital Presence:** High-converting web application with easy booking and service history tracking.
*   **WhatsApp Marketing:** Direct engagement and automated reminders to ensure high customer retention.
*   **Local Partnerships:** Collaborating with hotels and car rental agencies in Zanzibar.
*   **Referral Program:** Incentivizing existing customers to refer friends and family.

## 7. Operations Plan
*   **Phase 1:** Launch 2 mobile service units covering Stone Town and surrounding areas.
*   **Phase 2:** Expand coverage to the entire island of Zanzibar.
*   **Technology:** Utilize the custom Admin Dashboard for real-time dispatch, invoice generation, and maintenance logging.
*   **Staffing:** Certified mechanics trained in both technical skills and premium customer service.

## 8. Financial Highlights (Year 1)
*   **Low Overhead:** No expensive physical garage rental; focus on mobile equipment and tech.
*   **High Retention:** Automated reminders expected to increase repeat bookings by 40%.
*   **Scalability:** Business model allows for easy expansion by adding more mobile units as demand grows.
